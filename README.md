# The Tech Survival Journey

“You are not late to technology. You are simply starting your own journey.”

## About Me

I’m Hadeel, a veterinarian from Egypt. Due to certain circumstances, it was difficult for me to continue in my original field, but during the COVID-19 pandemic, I discovered medical interpretation and translation.

I trained in the field, worked with refugees through Tarjimly Inc., and built experience in medical translation.

Later, growing discussions about Artificial Intelligence made me curious rather than afraid.

That curiosity led me to start learning AI through the AICE program at ALX Africa.

## Why This Project Exists

This project is not about being an AI expert.

It is about my journey as a beginner trying to understand technology step by step.

I believe self-learning should not mean learning alone.

## What I Learned So Far

- Python basics  
- Machine learning  
- AI concepts and fundamentals  
- Jupyter Notebook  
- GitHub  
- Problem solving  
- Self-learning strategies  
- Using AI tools for learning  

## Beginner Survival Tips

- You are not late to technology. You are simply starting your own journey.  
- AI will not replace you. Someone who knows how to use AI will.  
- You do not need to know everything before you start.  
- Progress comes step by step.  
- Asking questions is part of learning.
